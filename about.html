About This Project

The story, data sources, and methodology behind Legends XI.

What is Legends XI?

Legends XI is a football hall of fame and live scouting hub built to celebrate the players who shaped the sport's history, while also giving fans a window into current player and team data through a live API.

Data Sources

The Hall of Fame section features 15 originally written profiles of legendary footballers, researched and authored specifically for this project. The Live Scout section pulls real-time player and team data from the API-Football service.

Built With

Semantic HTML5, hand-written CSS3 with Flexbox, Bootstrap 5 components, and vanilla modern JavaScript (ES6 classes and modules) — no frameworks, no jQuery.
